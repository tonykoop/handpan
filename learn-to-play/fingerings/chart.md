## Stroke techniques
| Stroke | Finger | Sound |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Ding | Thumb or ring fingertip | Deep, resonant, long sustain |
| Tone | Fingertip (index or middle) at field center | Clear singing tone |
| Tak/slap | Flat of finger, quick release | Short percussive click |
| Bass slap | Whole hand, muffle after strike | Low, muted field tone |

## Field-to-note map (D Kurd, clockwise from lower left)
A3 → Bb3 → C4 → D4 → E4 → F4 → G4 → A4 (→ back to A3)
