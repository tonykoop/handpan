# Ding Meditation — Songsheet

**Tempo:** Free  **Meter:** Free  **Difficulty:** ★☆☆

## Session structure (15 minutes)
| Minutes | Activity |
|---------|----------|
| 0–3 | Ding alone: 8 strokes, listening to full decay |
| 3–6 | Ding + one field: explore all 8 fields with ding sustain |
| 6–9 | Field circle walk: one note per breath, ascending |
| 9–12 | Ding as pulse: improvise freely over slow ding heartbeat |
| 12–15 | Ding alone: 8 strokes again, listen for differences |

The handpan rewards unhurried attention. These patterns are the foundation of all advanced playing.
