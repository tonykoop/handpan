# Kurd Dawn — Songsheet

**Key:** D Kurd (D natural minor)  **Tempo:** ♩= 80  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★★  
**Range:** A3–D5  **License:** MIT (original — Heifer Zephyr)

## Music
```
X:4 T:Kurd Dawn M:4/4 L:1/8 Q:1/4=80 K:Dmin
D4 A,4 | D4 F4 | A4 G4 | F8 |
A4 c4 | d4 c4 | A4 G4 | E8 |
G4 F4 | E4 D4 | A,4 c4 | D8 |
F4 A4 | c4 d4 | A4 F4 | D8 |
```

## Character
Four phrases of gradual emergence and resolution. The piece opens with the ding (D3) and rises
to D5 at the emotional peak of phrase 2, then gradually returns to the D foundation.
Each half note and whole note should ring completely — this is music for space, not fill.

## Handpan technique notes
- Opening: right-hand fingertip on D4 field, left thumb on ding (D3) simultaneously.
- Phrase 2 peak: the d4 (D5) may require reaching to the upper register of the field circle.
- Long notes: resist the urge to add extra strikes — silence is part of the composition.
- End: final D8 (whole note) = let the ding ring completely into silence.
