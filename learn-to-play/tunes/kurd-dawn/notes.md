# Notes: Kurd Dawn

**Composition:** Original tune for handpan in D Kurd. MIT licensed — Heifer Zephyr.
Written to use the full field circle of the D Kurd scale, from A3 ding up to D5 (if available)
or A4 on the standard 8-field configuration.

**Range check:** A3–D5. If your handpan only has 8 fields (up to A4), the D5 (d' in ABC)
can be replaced with A4 (an octave lower) — it changes the peak slightly but maintains the shape.

**Practice tips:**
1. Begin session with the warm-up before this tune.
2. The half notes (4 eighth-notes = 2 beats) are your breathing space — don't rush them.
3. This is an advanced tune because of the cross-field navigation and sustained tone control.
4. Try recording yourself and listening back — handpan resonance blends beautifully on recording.
