# Notes: Greensleeves (D Kurd Adaptation)

**Provenance:** Traditional English ballad, documented from 1580. PD.
This adaptation substitutes Bb for B natural to fit the D Kurd scale. The melody remains
recognizable; the modal shift creates a slightly more melancholic quality.

**Range check:** A3–C5. The C5 (c') is the highest note — confirm your A4 field (field 8)
is reachable without crossing over other fields.

**Practice tips:**
1. The 3/4 feel should be a gentle, swaying waltz — not a march.
2. Practice the verse and chorus as separate units.
3. The dotted rhythms (F3/2 G/) = dotted quarter + eighth. Count 1-and-2, not 1-2-3.
