# Notes: Sakura Sakura (Handpan)

**Provenance:** Traditional Japanese koto melody, Edo period. PD worldwide.
The famous "In" scale (A Bb D E) is a subset of your D Kurd scale — 4 of 8 fields.

**Range check:** A3–E4. Uses only fields 1 (A3), 2 (Bb3), 4 (D4), 5 (E4).

**Practice tips:**
1. Start by learning which field is A3, Bb3, D4, and E4 by touch alone.
2. The A3→Bb3 half-step is the signature tension of this melody — feel it.
3. The final A4 (whole note) can be played on the A4 field (upper octave) for a brighter ending.
