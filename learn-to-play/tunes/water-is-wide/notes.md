# Notes: The Water Is Wide (Handpan)

**Provenance:** Traditional English/Scottish ballad, PD. This arrangement sits in D natural minor
to match the D Kurd scale. The key (originally often G or F major) has been transposed to D.

**Range check:** A3–G4. All notes are on the 8 standard tone fields of D Kurd.

**Practice tips:**
1. The melody requires navigating from the low A3 and D4 fields up to G4 — map these positions first.
2. The dotted quarter + eighth figures (D3/2 F/) should flow lyrically, not mechanically.
3. This is the most expressive tune in the set — play it with feeling and slight tempo flexibility.
