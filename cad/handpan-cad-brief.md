# CAD Brief - Handpan Prototype

## Scope

Model the handpan as an inspection and communication assembly: top shell, bottom shell, rim joint, Gu port, Ding zone, and eight layout zones. Do not model tuned tone fields as final manufacturable pitch geometry.

## Model Requirements

- Units: inches primary, metric secondary.
- Global variables: shell_diameter_in = 21, shell_height_in = 9.5, steel_thickness_mm = 1.0, gu_diameter_in = 3.5.
- Datums: rim plane, shell center axis, 12 o'clock clock reference, Gu center.
- Configurations: top shell layout, bottom shell Gu, assembled shell, exploded review view.
- Tone fields: surface decals/sketch envelopes only, named by note and target frequency.
- Properties table: material path, source shell, measured thickness map reference, mass.

## Review Notes

CAD is not the acoustic authority for final handpan pitch. Its job is to keep the shop aligned on shell geometry, tooling interfaces, templates, and validation points.
