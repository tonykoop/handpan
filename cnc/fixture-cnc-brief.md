# CNC Brief - Fixtures And Templates

## Parts

1. Tuning support ring for 21 in shell.
2. Removable Ding template.
3. Eight oval tone-field layout templates.
4. Measurement overlay with strike and microphone reference marks.

## Materials

- Support ring: steel, plywood, or MDF prototype; final frame should be stiff and padded.
- Templates: acrylic, hardboard, plywood, or printed film.

## Constraints

- CNC parts support layout and measurement only.
- Do not cut, stamp, or machine final tone fields based solely on the CSV dimensions.
- Edges contacting the shell must be padded or polished.
- Mark all templates with note name, target Hz, and `layout estimate`.

## Output Files To Generate Later

- DXF/SVG support ring profile.
- DXF/SVG template sheet.
- PDF inspection overlay.
